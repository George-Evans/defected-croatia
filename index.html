Dear family, Firstly, thank you for being part of our festival in Tisno. I know that many of you travelled long distances to join us and I truly hope that the journey was worthwhile.

Every year brings more and more people together and I am absolutely certain you made new friends. Myself, I met people from Columbia, Japan, USA, Norway, Australia, Holland, Israel, Canada, Hungary, Brazil, France, New Zealand and of course, from all over the UK. We also had our biggest ever attendance from Croatia… people that truly appreciated the fact that we host the festival in Tisno. House music truly is a global movement. All the DJs made special comment on how amazing it was to play for you all, they came with their A-game and delivered because they fed off the atmosphere that you created 🙌🙌🙌.

The feedback from you guys has also been amazing, with many of you asking about 2019. I say this, as apparently there was a rumour that we did not plan to return next summer. Clearly this is not true. The event will run across the same week next year, so those that prefer to plan way in advance can start booking your accommodation and travel.

With that being said, we we will shortly be releasing a limited amount of Early Bird tickets. We expect these to sell out quickly and suggest that you don’t sleep on them!

We are also determined to maintain the family vibe that makes the event so unique. With that in mind we will also be limiting the overall sales for 2019.

Next summer will be even better. You may know by now that Defected will be celebrating 20 years this January and we will be partying right through until next August. We aim to make the party extra special, as we can think of nothing better than celebrating with our friends and family from around the world.

In the meantime I would love to thank you all again for loving our music and partying with us when we play around the world.

I look forward to seeing you all next August, if not before.

Simon Dunmore
Defected Records
