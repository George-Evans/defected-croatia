FAQ

Tickets

How Do I Collect My Tickets Or Wristbands?

You should receive a confirmation email from ticket seller you purchased your tickets from. Please print out your ticket or download it onto your phone. Bring your ticket along with your photo ID to the venue box office onsite at The Garden Resort, you will then be given a wristband.

My Tickets Have Not Arrived, Where Are They?

We do not post tickets. You should receive a confirmation email with your tickets attached. Please print out your ticket or download it onto your phone. If you haven’t received a ticket confirmation please email:

croatia@defected.com

Do I Need Photo ID?

You may be asked to show photo ID when collecting wristbands at the venue box office.

What Time Does The Box Office At Garden Resort Open/Close?

The box office opens each day at 12pm. Closing times vary – please check our website nearer the time for closing times.

Can My Friend Collect My Tickets From The Onsite Box Office?

No, all festival goers must collect their ticket in person with photo ID. Please ensure that all bookings made with multiple attendees have the correct details of each attendee so that they can collect them individually. Name changes can be made by logging in to your account and following the instructions on how to change a name.

I Booked Tickets For Friends/Family And My Name Is Printed On All The Tickets. Will My Friends And Family Still Be Able To Get Into The Festival?

Yes, so long as you are present to collect the tickets.

Are The Boat Parties Included In The Ticket Price?

No – tickets for these are sold separately. Boat Party tickets will be announced closer to the festival. Follow us on Facebook, Instagram and Twitter to keep updated.

I Can’t Go Anymore – Can I Get A Refund?

Unfortunately not. However, you can pass the ticket onto a friend. Name changes can be made by logging in to your account and following the instructions on how to change a name.

I Can’t Go Anymore - Can I Sell My Tickets?

Yes, you can pass the ticket onto a friend or sell them privately.

I Have Ordered More Than One Ticket, But I Can Only Seem To Print 1 Ticket. Will The Bar Code Confirm The Correct Number Of Tickets That I Have Purchased?

Once you have booked your ticket(s), you should receive an email confirmation containing an attachment for each ticket that you booked. If you haven’t received a confirmation please email:

croatia@defected.com

I Have A Wristband For A Boat Party, But Not For The Main Festival. Will I Be Allowed Into The Boat Party?

No, you will need a ticket to the main festival before you are able to attend one of the boat parties or after parties.

I Cannot Print My Ticket. Can I Bring My Ticket On My Phone?

Yes, but please ensure you have downloaded the ticket onto your phone so that the barcode can be scanned.

Can I Use The Barcode On My Phone To Enter The Festival, Boat Party Or Evening Events?

Please take your ticket to the box office when you arrive. You can either print your ticket or download it onto your phone. Your ticket will then be exchanged for a wristband. Don’t forget you will also need photo ID.

I Need An Extra Ticket To A Sold Out Event. Can You Help Me Find One?

Please email us and we’ll do our best to help:

croatia@defected.com

I Purchased Tickets For The Wrong Event, Can I Exchange My Tickets?

Please email us and we’ll do our best to help:

croatia@defected.com

I Don’t Arrive Until After The Garden Resort Box Office Closes. Can I Buy Tickets On The Door At Barbarellas?

Yes, the box office at Barbarellas is open from 11pm to 4am.

I’m Not Staying At The Garden Resort. Can I Leave The Festival And Return Later?

Yes, but please make sure you have your wristband on you at all times, as this will enable you to regain entry to the festival.

Barbarella's

WHAT IS BARBARELLAS?

Barbarellas is the official after party that takes place from 11pm – 6am. The club is located 10-15 minutes from the Garden Resort. We provide free shuttle buses to and from the club.

On-Site At The Garden Resort

WHERE IS THE LOST PROPERTY HELD?

In the ‘main reception’ for the apartments or the ‘info point’ of The Garden Resort.

WHERE IS THE CLOSEST ATM?

There is an ATM machine on the festival site, in the main reception area of the apartments. In addition there are 8 other ATMs in the centre of Tisno, which is about a 10-15 minute walk from The Garden Tisno.

WHAT ARE THE PRICES OF DRINKS ON THE SITE?

The bars have not fixed their prices for this season yet, but last year a 1/2 litre of draught beer was 30 kn and cocktails 30 kn – 70 kn. Wines, spirits and soft drinks are of course available too. reception area of the apartments. In addition there are 8 other ATMs in the centre of Tisno, which is about a 10-15 minute walk from The Garden Tisno.

DO THE BARS & SHOPS ON SITE AT GARDEN RESORT ACCEPT CREDIT AND DEBIT CARDS?

Yes, the only cards not accepted are American Express and Diners Club Card.

ARE THERE ANY PUBLIC SHOWERS OR CHANGING FACILITIES AT GARDEN RESORT THAT FESTIVAL TICKET HOLDERS CAN USE?

Private changing facilities are reserved for the use of Campers and Glampers. However, there is an open air shower on the beach and a public toilet for general use.

Eating & Drinking

WHERE IS THERE TO EAT?

Onsite at The Garden Resort there is the trattoria Magnolia which is open from breakfast till late and also wraps and sandwiches at the beach food area. In Tisno village there are lots of konobas to choose from, serving a selection of local and international cuisine.

CAN WE BRING OUR OWN FOOD AND DRINK ONTO THE SITE AND BOAT PARTIES?

No, you can’t, but there are plenty of bars selling cocktails, beers, wines and soft drinks and also food is available too.

TRAVEL

I’M NOT STAYING CLOSE TO THE FESTIVAL – IS THERE A LATE NIGHT TAXI SERVICE?

Metered taxis can be found at the entrance to the festival site in the main car park.

CAN I HIRE A CAR?

Yes, as long as you are over 21 years old and have held a licence for minimum of 1 year you can hire a car. Argus Car Hire & Uni Rent both offer car hire in Croatia.

www.arguscarhire.comwww.uni-rent.net

WHAT IS THE TRAIN AND BUS SERVICE LIKE IN CROATIA?

Basically, forget trains! The buses are altogether quicker, cheaper and connect all cities with a regular service. You can buy tickets on board the bus, but you should get a receipt. Longer journeys have service stops included in the travel time.
